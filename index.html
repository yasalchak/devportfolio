About Me

I graduated from Griffith University, Brisbane in 2017 with a bachelor of electronic and biomedical engineering. I then commenced a Ph.D in electronic and sports engineering in March 2018. I submitted my Ph.D thesis in October 2021 and since June 2021 have been working as a full-time researcher at Griffith University's centre of biomedical and rehabiliation engineering.
